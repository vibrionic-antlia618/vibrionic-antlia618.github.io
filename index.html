🛠️ Troubleshooting

If the program does not appear in your editor, restart the hosting application. Sometimes the editor needs a fresh boot to find new plugins. Verify that your graphics card drivers have the latest updates, as the software relies on your GPU to render color changes quickly.

If text or buttons look blurry, check your Windows display scaling settings. Resetting your display scaling to 100% usually fixes interface issues. If you experience crashes, ensure you have enough free space on your primary hard drive. Temporary files require additional room during the export phase of your video projects.

📝 Common Terms

Color Grading: The process of changing the color of a video to create a specific mood or style.

LUT: A Lookup Table that applies a specific color transformation to your footage.

Film Stock: A specific type of physical film that the digital software mimics for a textured, cinematic result.

Plugin: A piece of software that adds specific features to an existing program like a video editor.
